02 The engine

Four layers. One verdict.

A quantitative pipeline stacked to isolate penalty probability — not team form, not total goals. Penalties only.

L01 Referee intelligence

1,842 referee profiles. Penalty frequency, strictness variance, home/away bias — weighted into an arbitral factor.

L02 Pressure & Vulnerability

PPI measures offensive pressure on the box. DVS measures defensive fragility. Combined, they map the setup.

L03 Market synthesis

Internal fair-odds vs live bookmaker pricing. We push only when edge survives the spread.

Conviction-weighted units, bankroll-aware. The size of the bet scales with the quality of the signal.
